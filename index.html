Seminar Highlights

Singapore Security Eco-System

Dr. Steven WONG is an Associate Professor at the Singapore Institute of Technology (SIT) will highlight THE VARIOU​S ASPECTS OF THE CYBERSECURITY ECOSYSTEM, INCLUDING EDUCATION, COMMUNITY, INDUSTRY and REGIONAL OPPORTUNITIES. students can get involved and contribute to the Singapore cybersecurity eco-system including some of AiSP’s past/current activities. 

Steven Wong, Associate Professor at Singapore Institute of Technology

Future of Penetration Testing

In this talk TJ will explain the importance of conducting manual testing and to explain why it is important to understand the tools you use in your operations. In addition, TJ will also provide tips and resources to verify the tools you use and provide guidance on how you can create your own tools.

Tony Puntureiro

Evolving Landscape of Cloud Security

As more enterprise workloads move to the cloud, there's a need to understand security to the cloud, security on the cloud and security in the cloud. Edwinder Singh will provide further insights on the trending cloud solutions used by many companies in the world.

Edwinder Singh, Global Hybrid Cloud Advisory Director, NTT Ltd.

Car Hacking 101: How to get started

The thought of having a hacker remotely seizing control of your vehicle sounds like an action film. However, car hacking is defeinitely real and it is seeping into our daily lives. alina, founder of car security quarter(CSQ) will introduce the widely discussed topic.

Alina Tan, Founder of Car Security Quarter(CSQ)

Threat Intelligence & Visibility

Threat intelligence have been used to understand a threat actor's motives, targets and attack behaviours. Andrew will share his insights on How digital data is leveraged as "Threat Intelligence" and its impact on a daily basis.

Andrew Wong, Regional Sales Manager - Cyberint

Factors of Strong Security Architectures

For many enterprise architecture decision makers, a major challenge is trying to balance being stack-agnostic, being modern, and being secure. However, it is not a tradeoff in that one may only pick two of the three. In this talk, we will try to develop a mental framework to help design modern architectures and look at some of the key elements that make them secure, while preserving other desirable system properties.

Rayden Chia, Security Engineer - Google

Diaries of a Google employee

MEET PEI SI, A HARDWARE OPERATIONS ENGINEER UNDER GOOGLE EMPLOYEMENT. PEI SI WILL INTRODUCE THE IMPORTANCE OF IT AND SHARE INDUSTRY RELATED EXPERIENCES. THIS SESSION WILL HIGHLIGHT HOW GOOGLE BENEFITS FROM EMPLOYING MODERN TECHNOLOGY INTO THEIR COMPANY AS WELL AS THE SIGNIFICANCE OF CYBER SECURITY.

Pei Si, Hardware Operations Engineer - Google

The Art of Information Gathering

Red teaming helps to measure the organization's readiness by simulating real life attacks. To outsmart our enemies, one way is to think like them! During the session, Glenice Tan  will be sharing the initial phases of red teaming, in particular, Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) and Social Engineering. cause and effect of various online frauds will be shared as well to further understand the importance of internet footprints. 

Glenice Tan,  Associate Cybersecurity Specialist - Cyber Security Group Government Technology Agency
