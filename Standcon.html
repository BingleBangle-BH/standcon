Speakers of the galaxy

Singapore Security Eco-System

Professor Steven wong from Singapore institute of technology will highlight THE VARIOU​S ASPECTS OF THE CYBERSECURITY ECOSYSTEM, INCLUDING EDUCATION, COMMUNITY, INDUSTRY and REGIONAL OPPORTUNITIES. students can get involved and contribute to the Singapore cybersecurity eco-system including some of AiSP’s past/current activities. 

Penetration Testing

AI-powered penetration solution allows software developers to conduct smart hacking. Tony aka tjnull, a champion in the world of red teaming will introduce and explain the driving force behind such ai. This session will also highlight that the almighty ai may still be bested with manual techniques.

Diaries of a Google emplyee

Meet pei si, a hardware operations engineer under google employement. Pei si will introduce the importance of it and share industry related experiences. This session will highlight how google benefits from employing modern technology into their company as well as the significance of cyber security.

Car Hacking

The thought of having a hacker remotely seizing control of your vehicle sounds like an action film. However, car hacking is defeinitely real and it is seeping into our daily lives. alina, founder of car security quarter(CSQ) will introduce the widely discussed topic.
